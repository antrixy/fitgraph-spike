About MacroFactor

At MacroFactor, we translate the latest and greatest research and next-gen technology into powerful tools for every stage of your fitness journey.

Our apps, MacroFactor Nutrition and MacroFactor Workouts, help more than 500,000 users track their nutrition, log workouts in the gym, and achieve and maintain their fitness goals.

About MacroFactor Nutrition

MacroFactor is a smart macro tracker and nutrition coach. You can use it to track your weight and the foods you eat. The app then tells you your daily energy expenditure (the calories you burn) and your weight trend, and it adjusts your calories/macros to help keep you on track toward my goals.

Here are 4 reasons MacroFactor is the BEST app for losing weight, gaining muscle, and maintaining results:

  1. You can log foods faster in MacroFactor than in any other app. (We quantified it.) Plus, all items in MacroFactor’s food search database have been verified, so you can trust the accuracy of the foods you log.
  2. Its smart coaching feature will give you science-based recommendations tailored to your goals and preferences. It will also give you weekly updates to your macros, just like a nutrition coach would, so you can be sure you’re always on track toward your goals.
  3. The app’s algorithm uses your weight and nutrition data to offer a personalized energy expenditure estimate that is much more accurate than the wearables or static calculators that other apps rely on.
  4. On top of all the expected utilities you get in most food loggers (like barcode scanning and custom foods/recipes), MacroFactor also offers progress photos and body metrics tracking, a full suite of micronutrient tracking features, widgets, a label scanner, and more.

About MacroFactor Workouts

MacroFactor Workouts is a smart, dynamic workout tracker. It includes an intuitive interface for planning and logging your workouts, personalized workout programs, auto-progression to keep you on track, and much more.

Here are 3 reasons MacroFactor Workouts is the BEST app for training, tracking, and progressing in the gym.

  1. Sleek interface with serious power. Workouts is the perfect blend of power and simplicity. Logging sets, creating workouts, and navigating the app is a breeze. But you’ll also find powerful tools to track every detail – including rest time, drop sets, RIR, failure sets, partial reps, and much more.

  2. Science-based programming that learns and adapts to you. Our programs are engineered by experts and grounded in science. After your first workout, the app begins learning your rate of progress and strength/endurance relationship to adjust your program, helping you reach your goals and avoid plateaus.

  3. Total customization over your training experience. Workouts gives you total mastery over your training experience. You can personalize your program structure, adjust periodization, create custom exercises, set personalized rest timers, and manage different gym profiles.

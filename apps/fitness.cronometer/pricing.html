CRONOMETER GOLD FEATURES

Ad-free nutrition tracking from A to Zinc

Kick your health tracking up a notch with exclusive features for Gold Subscribers.

Photo Log

Snap. Edit. Log. See your meals and their nutrition breakdowns through the lens of your camera.

Voice Log

No interruption logging. Simply speak to us or Siri to log your meals.

Custom Charts

Highlight the data that matters most to you from macronutrients to vitamins and minerals.

Nutrition Scores

See how your diet impacts essential health markers including vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and more.

Recipe Importer

Paste a recipe URL into the Recipe Importer to automatically create a Custom Recipe with ingredients, measurements, and verified nutritional data.

Repeat Items

Don't just plan your meals—automate them. Schedule specific foods, meals, or recipes to automatically appear in your food diary daily, weekly, or on specific days.

Features that take you from plateau to progress

Macro
Scheduler

Customize your macronutrient targets for different days of the week with just a few taps. Set different templates and adjust your targets as needed.

Fasting
Timer

Start, stop, edit, and schedule your fasts directly from your food diary.

Oracle Nutrient Search

Missing magnesium in your diet? Low on iron? The Oracle Nutrient Search will suggest foods that are good sources of specific nutrients you’d like more of.

Custom
Biometrics

Create biometrics to keep track of your unique health markers like allergies, pain symptoms, test results, and more.

*Do you provide refunds?

We’re committed to your satisfaction. If you’re not fully satisfied, we offer a no-questions-asked refund within 30 days of purchase. This guarantee applies to one refund per product per customer and only for purchases made directly on our website.

Are the programs reusable?

Even better! The program will adapt over time to become increasingly customized based on your feedback.

What if I don't have the equipment at my gym?

You get to choose the exercises in the app, so if you have limited equipment, it is easily customizable to fit your specific needs.

Do I have to design my own programs?

You can select one of our 45 pre-built programs and let it autofill the exercises, or create your own plan from scratch! The app was built with flexibility in mind giving our users complete control!

Is the diet app included in this bundle?

No, the hypertrophy training bundle does not include the diet app. It does have an exclusive huge discount to save on the diet app though!

Is the app in the app stores?

Yes! The RP Hypertrophy app is available in the United States in the iOS app store. It will be in the US Google Play Store shortly and internationally following that.

This is also a web based application, which means it is accessed via a browser on your device. You simply log in, and after you’re in, save the page to your home screen, which will be like any other app on your device going forward.

The good news is, because it’s also a web app you can access it from any device with internet and a browser, not limiting you to just one device.

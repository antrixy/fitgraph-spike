FAQ

Will I get a free trial period when I sign up for an annual membership?

No. Annual memberships do not come with a free trial. If you have not subscribed to Zwift before, you can cancel your membership within 30 days for a refund.

How does the annual membership work?

You will be charged the full membership price at the time you sign up, and then will be charged yearly thereafter until you cancel.

Can I buy an annual membership for someone else?

You cannot purchase an annual membership on behalf of another member. The Zwift account that is logged in at the time of purchase will be the account that receives the annual membership.

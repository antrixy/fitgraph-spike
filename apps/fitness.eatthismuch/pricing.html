Get Started In 4 Easy Steps

  1. Create

    Tell us about yourself, your diet preferences, and your goals. We'll create meal plans specific to your needs in seconds! You always have the option to tweak your settings later.

  2. Collect

    Review your plan to make sure everything looks good, swapping out anything you don't like. Then, use the grocery list to shop for your ingredients.

    You can get your groceries delivered from Instacart, Amazon Fresh, Walmart, and more to save time!

  3. Cook

    Enjoy making and eating delicious meals without the stress of planning. Not only will you know you're eating better, you'll have more time and energy for other things.

  4. Conquer!

    Make adjustments to your preferences, discover new meals, or put your favorites on repeat. Review nutrition stats, track weight progress, and achieve your goals!

An independent research study showed that structured meal plans and grocery lists were the most important factor for people to adopt a diet and stick to it long term. Our own internal research has shown that endless, delicious recipes are the biggest reason people find our meal planning so enjoyable. :)

Everything You Need To Stay On Track

Flexible Nutrition Targets

Great For Keto and Carb Cycling

Complex nutrition or macro schemes? Never fear, we're here for you. Set different targets for each day of the week and make quick adjustments if you miss a day.

Quick Ingredient Filters

Great For Vegetarians and Allergies

Keyword filters ensure you only see foods that fit your specific diet or meal plan needs. You can also block specific ingredients if you see something you just don't like.

Easy Recurring Foods

Great For Building Habits

Recurring dishes allow you to put your favorite foods and meals on repeat. Following a meal plan is easier when you keep a few familiar meals in your rotation, and our generator will work around them.

Simple Recipe Collections

Great For Organizing Your Favorites

Create collections of recipes to help you organize your favorite recipes, or browse popular collections created by our community.

Endless Regeneration

Great For Finding Something New

Cost-Saving Pantry

Great For Everyone

Our pantry lets you track everything you have on hand for meals so you know when foods (especially the preservative-free ones!) need to be replaced. Easily see meal suggestions that use up what's in your pantry.
